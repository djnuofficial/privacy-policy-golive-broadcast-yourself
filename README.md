    <p><strong>GoLive | Broadcast Yourself</strong> ("we," "our," or "us"), developed by <strong>Nu World Entertainment</strong>, is committed to protecting the privacy of our users. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our mobile application. By downloading and using GoLive, you consent to the practices described herein.</p>

    <h2>1. Information We Collect and Process</h2>
    <p>Because GoLive does not require a user account or sign-in to utilize either its core free features or premium tiers, our data collection is strictly minimal, transient, and targeted solely at operational functionality.</p>
    <ul>
        <li><strong>Audio and Microphone Access:</strong> To stream live audio to your destination radio station, the application explicitly requires permission to access your device’s microphone. We do not record, store, archive, or analyze your audio. Audio data is processed entirely in real-time as a transient data stream sent directly to your station's playout system.</li>
        <li><strong>Station Credentials:</strong> To connect securely to your station's web-based playout infrastructure, you must input your designated station credentials. This information is held locally or processed securely via authentication protocols exclusively to validate and establish the live stream session.</li>
        <li><strong>Device and Network Analytics:</strong> We detect and evaluate connection metrics (such as shifting between 4G, 5G, and Wi-Fi networks) to optimize audio latency, buffer stability, and broadcast resilience in unpredictable outdoor or high-density environments.</li>
        <li><strong>Subscription and Payment Processing:</strong> Premium features are managed directly via third-party application stores (e.g., Apple App Store or Google Play Store). We do not collect, view, or store financial credentials or credit card numbers. All transaction telemetry is securely handled by the native platform store.</li>
    </ul>

    <h2>2. Third-Party Advertising and Mediation Networks</h2>
    <p>To keep the core features of GoLive free to use, the free tier of the application is supported by advertisements. We integrate third-party advertising services, specifically <strong>Google AdMob</strong> alongside other third-party mediation ad networks.</p>
    <p>These advertising partners may automatically collect and utilize specific diagnostic and device identifiers to personalize, serve, and measure relevant ad campaigns. This data may include, but is not limited to, your device's Advertising ID (such as Apple's IDFA or Google's Advertising ID), IP address, internet service provider, and basic device configuration metrics. You can manage your ad tracking preferences directly within your device’s native operating system privacy settings.</p>

    <h2>3. Data Distribution and Sharing</h2>
    <p>We do not sell, rent, or lease your personal data. Information is only shared with third-party components vital to operating the service:</p>
    <ul>
        <li><strong>Your Radio Station Playout System:</strong> Your real-time audio stream and authentication headers are transmitted solely to the destination control room that you explicitly configure and initiate.</li>
        <li><strong>Ad Networks and Mediation Partners:</strong> Anonymous device metadata is processed by programmatic advertising entities to sustain our free-tier model.</li>
        <li><strong>App Store Operators:</strong> Transaction and subscription management are shared exclusively with the platform ecosystem provider through which you initiated the premium subscription.</li>
    </ul>

    <h2>4. Children's Privacy</h2>
    <p>GoLive is designed and engineered exclusively for professional radio broadcasters, field reporters, and media personnel. It is not intended for, marketed to, or designed for use by individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly gather any data from children.</p>

    <h2>5. Compliance and International User Rights</h2>
    <p>Depending on your geographic location, you may possess specific statutory data protections under privacy regimes such as the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) in the European Union or the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) in the United States. Because we collect zero identifying personal profiles or permanent sign-in credentials, our storage footprint is natively restricted. If you have any inquiries regarding exercising your localized rights (such as data portability or deletion request inquiries), you may reach out directly to our support channel.</p>

    <h2>6. Revisions to This Policy</h2>
    <p>We reserve the right to modify this Privacy Policy at any time to reflect software changes or legal evolution. Any adjustments will take effect instantly upon the policy's publication inside the app storefront or application setup pages. Continued use of the software constitutes ongoing agreement to the active policy.</p>
